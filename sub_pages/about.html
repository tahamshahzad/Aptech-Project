About Us & Our Goal

Liberty Electronics is one the the most established electronic retailer of the world. The company was founded in 1976 by our esteemed visionary Mr. SpongeBob Squarepants. The company transitioned from a private limited company to a public limited company in 2001, and since then, it's only been uphill for the company, it's customers, and it's shareholders. The enterprise went on to establish it's roots in the international market. Upholding excellent service and a good name in the industry led to contracts from renowned manufacturers with the company. Liberty Electronics has been awarded "Best Sales Point Service Holder" award in the International TechCrunch Festival, California, 2010. Liberty Electronics aims to keep growing and expand further, to provide excellent service as it always have, and to please it's shareholders more every fiscal year.

Our Goal & Mission has always been and will be to provide our costumers with latest technology products at an affordable price, we always look forward to archive this by all possible means.
